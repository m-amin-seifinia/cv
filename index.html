ABOUT ME

Hello and thanks for visiting my personal website.

I'm Mohammad Amin Seifinia, I'm 26 and I'm from Zanjan, Iran. I am currently pursuing defending my thesis in the field of Energy Conversion, a branch of Mechanical Engineering. My field of study mostly involves researching the transfer of heat in between different environments and boundaries, especially in nanoscale domains. I'm also interested and have experience in working with optimization algorithms, artificial intelligence and machine learning (For further info, click the "Skills and interests" button in the navigation section).

Fast forwarding through the technicalities and academics, I'm a music afficionado, and a bit of an audiophile. My interest in music has lead me to learn Electric Guitar for the last 2 years (at the time of writing!). I also occasionally do pencil drawing, and also sometimes do digital art and experiment with colors. The website before your eyes is a result of one of my occasional dabbles in this pursuit. My other "hobby" includes sating my never-ending thirst for learning new things, as I can usually be found watching video courses and educational videos in different fields on the internet in my free time.

I'm always interested in working in teams, and believe that a cooperative-competitive environment is beneficial to all parties involved, so I'm willing to partake in team activites involving any of my fields of interests. I also love sharing and spreading science and knowledge, and I'm always willing to learn, teach and share knowledge, so if ANY query comes to your mind about basically anything on this website, I'd be more than happy to entertain a conversation with you!
